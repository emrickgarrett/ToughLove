The Problem

We live in a time where Food is a commodity, and Food Manufacturers do whatever they can to ensure you come back for seconds. For those who get on a period of eating, particularly fatty and sugary foods, it can become an addiction some doctors believe to be as strong as an addiction to Cocaine.

For those who fall into a pattern of eating, this fact makes it even harder to quit. And when Food has overtaken society and it's hard to drive a few blocks without seeing a Fast Food Restaurant.

Having the tools to motivate yourself to eat healthy and lose weight is difficult. Especially when results can take weeks before they become noticeable.
